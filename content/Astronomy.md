# Astronomy — Study Roadmap

A self-study path from broad survey to rigorous first-principles astrophysics.
Videos live in the **Media** section (added as courses, so progress is tracked
per lesson); this note is the map and my running notes.

## The path

1. **Crash Course Astronomy** — Phil Plait, 46 short episodes.
   The wide survey: sky motions, the solar system, stars, galaxies, cosmology.
   Fast, visual, no math. Good for building the mental map first.
   → [thecrashcourse.com/topic/astronomy](https://thecrashcourse.com/topic/astronomy)

2. **Yale ASTR 160 — Frontiers & Controversies in Astrophysics** — Charles Bailyn.
   ~24 full lectures. The rigorous pass: Kepler/Newton derivations, exoplanets,
   relativity, black holes, dark energy. Some math; slower, deeper.
   → playlist `PLD1515420F4E601A4`
